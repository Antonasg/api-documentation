DotKey

public struct DotKey: PathIndexer

Everything in indexable will explode keypaths, for example, foo.bar will become foo, bar should you have . nested in your JSON keys, use this class

[foo.bar: 2]

would be accessed data[DotKey(foo.bar)] this will preserve the .
